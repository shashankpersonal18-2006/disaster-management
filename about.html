Who We Are

We are a disaster management organization dedicated to providing rapid response and support during emergencies, ensuring safety, and minimizing risks through effective coordination and preparedness.

Importance of Disaster Management

  • Minimizes loss of life and property during disasters.
  • Ensures quick response and effective relief operations.
  • Improves community preparedness and resilience.
  • Coordinates efforts among government, NGOs, and volunteers.
  • Facilitates recovery and rehabilitation after disasters.
  • Promotes awareness and education on disaster risks and safety measures.
